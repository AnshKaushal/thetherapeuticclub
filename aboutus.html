What we are?

Hey! we are a group of students who want to make every person around us self relient and confident enough to share his/her thoughts with the world.

What we provide?

Students often lack the confidence to speak in public. We'll provide a stage to you where you'll be able to express your thoughts without any hesitation and fear.

What you'll need to do?

Usually other clubs have a fixed deposit system but we'll take fees just before the event! [ie; entry fees]. So if you wanna join the club, just message us at either our socials or using the contact form. we'll get back to you asap.
